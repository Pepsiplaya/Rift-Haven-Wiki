# Stats and Gear

Back: [Home](../index.md)

## 1) What this system is

Stats and gear determine your real power. Class level gives your baseline, and equipment, jewelry, talents, food, and potions push your final performance.

## 2) Who should use it

- Players optimizing combat output.
- Crafters targeting efficient upgrades.
- Group players balancing offense, defense, and utility.

## 3) Core rules (exact mechanics)

Primary stats:

- Strength: physical damage
- Agility: crit chance and dodge
- Dexterity: movement speed
- Intelligence: spell power
- Constitution: max health and regen
- Wisdom: healing effectiveness
- Spirit: magic resistance and crowd control reduction

Rarity tiers:

- Poor
- Common
- Uncommon
- Rare
- Epic
- Legendary

Armor type ladder:

- Cloth < Leather < Mail < Plate

Armor class limits:

- Fighter: Plate
- Paladin: Mail
- Rogue: Leather
- Elementalist: Cloth

Weapon restrictions:

- Fury Fighter: battleaxes
- Light Paladin: clubs
- Sol Elementalist: staffs
- Shinobi Rogue: swords

Jewelry slots:

- 2 rings
- 1 amulet

## 4) Jewelry and socketing

Jewelry is now a real part of progression.

Important rules:

- Rings and amulets give real stats and update live when equipped.
- Gems decide what stat the jewelry focuses on.
- Copper, silver, and gold bands create different strength levels.
- Voidstone is the spread gem and gives smaller bonuses across all primary stats instead of stacking one stat.

General progression:

- Copper is the basic line
- Silver is stronger
- Gold is the strongest standard jewelry line

General gem identity:

- Most gems focus a single primary stat
- Voidstone spreads smaller bonuses across all primary stats
- Jewelry stats update live when equipped or removed

## 6) Progression path

1. Match weapon type to class first.
2. Stay inside your class armor limit.
3. Add jewelry once your core armor/weapon path is stable.
4. Use food and alchemy to fill weak spots in your build.
5. Use permanent essence progression as a long-term layer, not a replacement for gear.
6. Upgrade stat identity instead of just chasing rarity blindly.

Suggested stat focus:

- Fury: Strength / Constitution
- Light: Wisdom / Constitution with support utility
- Sol: Intelligence / Spirit / Dexterity
- Shinobi: Agility / Dexterity / crit support

## 7) Common mistakes

- Equipping high-rarity gear with the wrong stat spread.
- Ignoring jewelry slots.
- Ignoring required-level checks.
- Building damage only and skipping survivability prep from food, potions, and defensive stats.
