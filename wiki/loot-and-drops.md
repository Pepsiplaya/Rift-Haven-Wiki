# Loot and Drops

Back: [Home](../index.md)

## 1) What this system is

This page covers the current creature-family drop direction and dungeon chest loot rules.

## 2) Core rules (exact mechanics)

General rules:

- Most non-undead, non-excluded creatures now drop an essence every kill
- Standard essence drops are usually `1-2`
- Side materials roll independently
- Side hides/materials usually roll `0-3`
- Side meat/food usually roll `0-2`
- Special single materials usually roll `0-1`

Main exclusions:

- Desert chickens: no drops
- Hatworm: no drops
- Undead: no general-material drops
- Void creatures keep their own existing drop identity and are not covered by the general rework

## 3) Beast and wildlife drop families

### Life family

- Rat -> Life Essence, Soft Hide
- Boar -> Life Essence, Soft Hide, Raw Wildmeat
- Ram -> Life Essence, Soft Hide, Raw Wildmeat
- Black Wolf -> Life Essence, Light Hide, Raw Chicken
- Rattle Snake -> Life Essence, Light Hide, Raw Chicken
- Grizzly Bear -> Life Essence, Medium Hide, Raw Pork
- Mosshorn -> Life Essence, Medium Hide, Raw Pork

### Lightning family

- Cave Spider -> Lightning Essence, Poison Sack
- Molerat -> Lightning Essence, Medium Hide
- Scarak Larva/Louse -> Lightning Essence, Raw Wildmeat
- Scarak Locust/Seeker -> Lightning Essence, Raw Wildmeat, Poison Sack, Chitin
- Scarak Warrior/Fighter -> Lightning Essence, Chitin
- Scarak Tank/Defender -> Lightning Essence, Chitin

### Broodmother

- Scarak Broodmother -> 5-10 Lightning Essence
- Can also roll:
- Raw Beef
- random gem
- Silver Bar
- random gear in the level 15-20 range

### Ice family

- Yeti -> Ice Essence, Heavy Hide, random gem

### Fire family

- Magma Toad -> Fire Essence, Medium Hide, Raw Pork
- Fire Dragon -> Fire Essence, Chitin, Raw Beef, random gem
- Firesteel Golem -> Fire Essence, Ancient Steel Scrap

### Water family

- Hedera / Hedera_KD -> Water Essence
- Cult Werewolf -> Water Essence, Heavy Hide
- Cult Knight -> Water Essence

### Void / dark-support family

- Outlander Priest -> Void Essence, Raven Feathers, random non-utility/non-legendary potion, 15% Voidstone chance
- Outlander Sorcerer -> Void Essence, Raven Feathers, random non-utility/non-legendary potion, 15% Voidstone chance

### Random essence family

- Shadow Knight_KD -> random essence roll

## 4) Dungeon chest loot

Current dungeon chest behavior:

- Common dungeon chests roll `1-6` items
- Legendary/gold dungeon chests roll `4-12` items

Common chest themes:

- Bronze bars
- Silver bars
- eggs
- useful foraging/cooking ingredients

Legendary chest themes:

- Silver bars
- Gold bars
- useful forage/cooking ingredients
- gems at a lower rate than bars/materials

Important note:

- Duplicate rolls can happen if RNG lands on the same item more than once
- Dungeon chest cleanup was done through asset-side chest pools, not by rewriting the dungeon plugin reward system

## 5) What this means for progression

- Creature farming now feeds professions much more directly
- Essences are part of normal world progression now
- Cooking, Alchemy, and Jewelcrafting all benefit more from farming specific creature families
- Dungeon chests are now better for bars/material support instead of being overloaded with junk or broken ids

## 6) Common mistakes

- Farming undead expecting the new essence/material loop
- Ignoring creature family type when hunting for a specific profession mat
- Expecting every legendary chest to be gem-heavy
