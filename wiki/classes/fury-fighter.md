# Fury Fighter

Back: [Classes Overview](../classes.md)

## 1) What this system is

Fury Fighter is the all-in front-line bruiser path. You trade range for relentless melee pressure, rage momentum, and heavy weapon control.

## 2) Who should use it

- Players who like direct melee combat.
- Players who want high pressure in close fights.
- Parties that need a durable front-line brawler.

## 3) Core rules (exact mechanics)

- Launch subclass: `Fury`.
- Signature weapon family: battleaxes.
- Talent tiers unlock every 5 levels.

## 4) Progression path

Recommended early build (balanced):

1. Level 5: `Blood Boil`
2. Level 10: `Sundering Shards`
3. Level 15: `Ferocious`
4. Level 20: `Never Ending Fury`
5. Level 25: `Unyielding`
6. Level 30: `Adrenaline Spin`

Alternative safer build:

1. Level 5: `Thick Skinned`
2. Level 10: `Sundering Shards`
3. Level 15: `Bloodied`
4. Level 20: `Goading Fury`
5. Level 25: `Unyielding`
6. Level 30: `Cyclone of the North`

## 5) Rewards and breakpoints

- Level 10 unlocks core pressure talents.
- Level 20 gives major throw/fury utility options.
- Level 30 decides your endgame spin identity (stun/rage/speed style).

## 6) Common mistakes

- Overcommitting without cooldown awareness.
- Ignoring survivability talents in long fights.
- Playing too far from heal/support in group content.
