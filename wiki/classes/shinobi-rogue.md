# Shinobi Rogue

Back: [Classes Overview](../classes.md)

## 1) What this system is

Shinobi Rogue is a mobile precision DPS path built around speed, crit pressure, and assassin-style kill windows.

## 2) Who should use it

- Players who like fast gameplay and burst timing.
- Players who enjoy repositioning and target isolation.
- PvP-focused players who want pick potential.

## 3) Core rules (exact mechanics)

- Launch subclass: `Shinobi`.
- Signature weapon family: swords and kunai style play.
- Core loop: mobility + crit setup + execute windows.

## 4) Progression path

Recommended early burst build:

1. Level 5: `Draining Jutsu`
2. Level 10: `Explosive Tag`
3. Level 15: `Assassin's Cunning`
4. Level 20: `Adrenaline`
5. Level 25: `Go for the Head`
6. Level 30: `Execution Jutsu`

Recommended control/survival build:

1. Level 5: `Swift Foot`
2. Level 10: `Throw of the Wind`
3. Level 15: `Quick Reflexes`
4. Level 20: `Caltrops`
5. Level 25: `Crippling Strike`
6. Level 30: `Mark for Death`

## 5) Rewards and breakpoints

- Level 10 introduces high-impact kunai upgrades.
- Level 20 opens invisibility/stat-burst/control branches.
- Level 30 gives your strongest finisher identity.

## 6) Common mistakes

- Fighting extended front-line brawls like a tank class.
- Wasting mobility on engage with no exit plan.
- Missing burst windows by not pre-setting crit and debuff effects.
